# Lessons Learned — MeshCentral Plugin

## Purpose
This file captures non-obvious discoveries, gotchas, and hard-won solutions.
If you figured something out that took more than 5 minutes and the next agent
will likely face the same issue, document it here.

## Rules
- Append new entries at the TOP (newest first)
- Each entry: date, one-line summary, then the detail
- Keep entries short (3-5 lines max)
- Once a lesson is stable and verified, promote it into Instructions.md
  and remove it from here
- Do NOT log routine work here (that goes in Daily_Logs)
- Do NOT log current state here (that goes in hot.md)

## Format
```
### YYYY-MM-DD: One-line summary
Detail of what happened, why it was surprising, and what to do instead.
```
